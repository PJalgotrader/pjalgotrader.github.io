U.S. Presidential Election Results by State

About This Visualization

This interactive map displays U.S. presidential election results by state across multiple election cycles. States are colored based on the winning party: blue for Democratic victories and red for Republican victories. The intensity of the color represents the margin of victory. Hover over states to see detailed results including vote counts, percentages, and electoral votes. Use the year selector to explore different election cycles and observe changing political landscapes over time.

Data Source: Federal Election Commission and Associated Press

Technologies: D3.js, TopoJSON, HTML5, CSS3
